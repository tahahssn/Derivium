Our Tools

Limits

Limits define the value a function approaches as the input comes close to a certain point. They are the base of calculus.

Derivatives

Derivatives measure the rate of change of a function. They are essential for finding slopes and solving optimization problems.

2D Graphs

2D Graphs visually represent mathematical functions on a flat plane using X and Y axes to show relationships clearly.

3D Graphs

3D Graphs represent complex mathematical relationships in three dimensions, helping visualize depth, surfaces, and volumes.
